The Design Choice

Built for stability.
Designed to earn trust.

Most AI agent systems compete to add more agents, more autonomy, more complexity. We went the other direction. Steward uses a deliberate hub-and-spoke architecture — because for the businesses we serve, predictability is the feature.

Hub
Steward — Your Chief of Staff
Every action, every escalation, every output flows through one central agent. Steward coordinates, delegates, and decides what reaches you. You always know who to talk to. The org always knows who's accountable.
Spokes
Specialist Agents
Scout (Growth), Harbor (Customer Ops), and Beacon (Legal Watcher) each own their domain completely. They don't compete with each other. They don't generate contradictory outputs. They operate within their defined scope and surface what genuinely requires a human decision.
The Philosophy
Stability Before Complexity
A fully autonomous, peer-to-peer agent network sounds impressive. It also produces conflicts, contradictions, and outputs that are hard to trace. Hub-and-spoke means every decision has a chain of custody. Every action is logged. When something breaks, you know exactly where and why.
This is not a V1 limitation. It is the V1 design.
How it works

From brief to running
org in weeks.

01
You define the operational problem
Tell us what your business needs done repeatedly, with judgment, at scale. Ops coordination, client delivery, compliance workflows, reporting — wherever your team spends time that follows patterns.
02
We design the org
We map your problem to a set of specialized agent roles. Each role is scoped precisely: domain, responsibilities, authority, escalation paths. The design is delivered before a single agent is deployed.
03
We deploy and run the initial sprint
Agents are stood up, briefed, and set to work. We run the first sprint alongside you — watching for gaps, mismatches, and calibration issues. Most corrections happen in week one.
04
The org runs
After calibration, the organization operates. You receive results, reports, and escalations that actually require your decision. Everything else happens without you initiating it.
