The Founder

Sees what's coming early. Builds it at scale. Now building again.

Yves La Rose has a track record of recognizing transformative technology before the consensus forms, and acting on it. Before crypto became an industry, he was already in it. He also spent years working for the Canadian Government and the Assembly of First Nations, where he learned to move institutions and align stakeholders in high-stakes, distributed environments. Over the last decade, he founded and led multiple large-scale technology initiatives, building organizations spanning dozens of countries, managing significant capital, and working directly with institutional and government stakeholders. Nether Labs is next: applying the same operational discipline to autonomous AI organizations.

"At some point you stop asking whether AI can run an organization and start asking why you haven't built one yet."

Yves La Rose, Founder, Nether Labs
