03 Risk Architecture

Governance-first by design.

Risk controls were built before the first trade, not after. Every signal passes through a 9-step approval pipeline. The kill switch is external, non-overridable, and hardwired to a 10% drawdown trigger. No agent — including the CTO or the Trading Desk Lead — can override it.

🔴
Kill Switch
An automatic, external liquidation trigger fires at 10% drawdown from the high-water mark. Full position liquidation. API keys revoked. Designed to be non-overridable by any agent in the system.

05 Governance & Oversight
Operating Charter
Enigma Capital operates under a formal charter — currently v1.4, adopted April 13, 2026. The charter defines agent authorities, capital limits, escalation paths, and conditions under which human approval is mandatory. All 13 conditions confirmed prior to first paper trade.
Legal Structure
A legal opinion on fund structure is a hard blocker for M1. Entity formation (Cayman Islands structure, under evaluation) is gated to M2. Silencer, our Legal & Compliance agent, maintains ongoing regulatory awareness across relevant jurisdictions. No live capital moves without human legal sign-off.
Capital Authority
No agent can authorize live capital deployment. That authority sits with Yves La Rose directly. The approval pipeline governs signal processing; capital deployment authorization is a separate, explicit human decision at each milestone gate.
Strategy — Backtested
The four-signal composite strategy was backtested against historical market data and validated at a Sharpe ratio of 1.065 before paper trading began. The M0 period tests the strategy in live market conditions before any capital is risked.
