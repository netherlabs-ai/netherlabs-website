What We're Building
Nether Labs parent · Enigma Capital quant division

Nether Labs is the parent company. Its first commercial product is Enigma Capital — a fully autonomous, institutional-grade quantitative trading organization run entirely by AI agents.

The org operates like any high-performing human firm: C-suite leadership, specialized execution teams, compliance gates, risk management, and a kill switch. The difference is every role is an AI agent — purpose-built, identity-defined, with clear scope and escalation paths.

Yves operates as founder and board. He interfaces with one person (the Chief of Staff), who coordinates the full swarm. Day-to-day operations run without his direct input.

🎯
Design Principles
Semantic Alpha
LLMs process unstructured data (news, sentiment, on-chain). Deterministic Python handles all execution. No LLM math, ever.
Hard Kill Switch
10% drawdown triggers full liquidation and API key revocation. Kill switch lives outside the agent swarm — cannot be overridden by any agent.
Milestone Funding
$1K proven before $10K. $10K proven before scaling. Each milestone requires formal sign-off from Oracle, Silencer, and Disruptor before capital increases.
Board-Only Principals
Yves and co-founder Ted Cahall act as Board of Directors only. No operational decisions — those belong to the swarm.
