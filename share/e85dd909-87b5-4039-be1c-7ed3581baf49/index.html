Trading Signal Framework
Active Alpha Signals — 4 Strategies Semantic Alpha · No HFT

Enigma Capital generates alpha from unstructured information — sentiment, macro flows, and behavioral signals that traditional quantitative funds underweight. LLMs process the data; deterministic Python executes the trades. No model makes math decisions. No model touches a live order without passing through the full approval pipeline.

😨
Fear & Greed Contrarian
Monitors the CNN Fear & Greed Index for extreme readings. In periods of extreme fear (<25), historically outperformed markets present a mean-reversion opportunity. The signal triggers a contrarian long when fear is at its highest and macro conditions confirm.
BEHAVIORAL · SENTIMENT
₿
BTC Dominance Rotation
Tracks Bitcoin's price relative to its 50-day moving average as a proxy for risk-on/risk-off positioning in crypto markets. IBIT exposure scales with BTC momentum relative to trend, capturing rotation flows between risk assets and crypto.
MACRO · MOMENTUM
🗞️
LLM News Sentiment
An LLM continuously processes crypto news headlines and classifies each article as bullish, bearish, or neutral. Aggregated sentiment scores create a directional signal that detects narrative shifts before they appear in price, providing early alpha.
NLP · INFORMATION EDGE
🔄
Exchange Net Flow
Monitors net Bitcoin inflows and outflows across major exchanges. Large net outflows (BTC leaving exchanges) historically precede price increases as supply tightens. Large inflows signal selling pressure. This on-chain signal provides supply/demand clarity.

About Nether Labs

Nether Labs is the parent organization behind Enigma Capital. Founded by Yves La Rose — who previously co-founded EOS Nation and led the EOS Network Foundation through its rebrand to Vaulta as CEO — Nether Labs is the operating entity where autonomous AI agent infrastructure is being developed and deployed.
